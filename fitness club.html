Information about fitness club


Sports centre and gym-:

At RCSI we understand that busy students need the very best facilities to balance their academic and health and fitness ambitions. Our fitness centre caters for complete beginners to regular exercisers and offers the very best gym equipment available anywhere in Ireland.

Personalised Advice-:

The gym team at RCSI, which consists of qualified and experienced fitness professionals, are always on hand to assist you in achieving your health and fitness goals in the most time-effective way. From tailored fitness programmes and nutrition advice to sports specific programmes, they are available at all times to ensure you get the very best results. If you need some extra motivation, we offer an extensive range of group fitness classes. From dance, yoga & mobility to kettlebells, circuits & spinning, there's sure to be something to interest you. Our extensive opening hours mean that we are open when it suits you - be it an early session to wake you up, a lunchtime workout to invigorate your afternoons' study or a late night class to finish your day strong.

Students-in-gym-:

Student in gym personal training Our cardiovascular suite has over 40 pieces of equipment to ensure you get the best variety in your workouts. To tone, build & sculpt your body, you'll need resistance machines & with our range of over 30 sectorised weight machines you will get the results your want in an effective & safe way.

Our weights room contains over 2,500kg of plates & dumb bells & a large range of racks, benches & platforms you can be sure of hitting your strength & conditioning goals. Our ladies only gym is a provate gym with cardiobascular & toning equipment provides a private area for ladies of all abilities & nationalities to workout.

Membership Agreement-:

To be eligible for membership, applicant must be: a current RCSI registered student of Surgical Training or Emergency Medicine. Applications for membership must be on the official application form provided for that purpose. If the applicant is under the age of 18 years, the application form must be signed by a parent or legal guardian. All members must swipe their RCSI ID card upon entering the gym & this applies to all visits. The membership term is for an initial period of 6/12 months depending on which membership option is selected. Following the initial minimum membership period membership you will be contacted to remind you about your renewal date. The membership renewal fee must be received at least one day before the membership expiry date to ensure continuation of the membership. Membership will automatically expire one day after the renewal date if no further payment is received. Renewal payments may be made at any time during the membership term. This will not affect the expiry date of the existing membership.
